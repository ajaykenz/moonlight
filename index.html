MOONLIGHT

"Recyling Metal, Reviving the Future"

Moonlight Industrial Supplies Factory, established in 2003, has emerged as a leading company in Qatar specializing in the collection, sorting, processing, and bailing of recycled ferrous and nonferrous metal scrap. Initially focused on metal serap collection and supply, our operations have expanded to include comprehensive processing capabilities. We employ advancedtechnologies and proprietary methods for sorting, crushing, grinding, briquetting, and granulating various types of scrap materials, ensurin high-quality recycled materials for manufacturing industries.

Over our 20-year journey, we have cultivated strong partnerships with metal plants and foundries both domestically and internationally. These collaborations enable us to provide superior products and maintain efficient supply chains. Our commitment to innovation and sustainability drives us to continuously improve and excel in the metal scrap industry.

Our vision is to be a global leader n the metal industry,known for our commitment to excellence, environmental responsibility, and innovative solutions. We envision a world where metals are harnessed sustainably, contributing to a brighter and more sustainable future for all.

What Products We Trade

Our metal recycling division is dedicated to environmental responsibility and sustainability. By processing discarded metal scrap, we reduce waste and conserve valuable resources, transforming waste into reusable raw materials that support industries and drive a more sustainable future.
